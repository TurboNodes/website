# Turbo does not consider itself a DePIN

> DePIN stands for *Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks*

Turbo is a distributed network but is not truely decentralized and that's actually okay!

## No Tokenomics


## The blockchain buzzword

No blockchain, our architecture is practical, not ideology/buzzword-driven, no need to bloat it.

It's okay to not be fully decentralized because the foundation of our trust model relies on the pieces of our system: secure connections, credential management, and verified payments.

These don't need complex cryptographic principles to be verified, just take a look a the [source code](https://github.com/L1shed/Turbo) and, if you're not happy, seft-host your own backend with docker compose.

## Cryptocurrencies

We proceed most of our payments in cryptocurrency for these main reasons:

- Global unicity, crypto is cross-border and censorship resistent
- Financial transparency and reliability, every transaction is stored inside a public blockchain. 

<br/>
Finally, we solve a problem that is: shipping data fast cheap worldwide. Blockchain is slow and requires heavy computations, so we forget it ... for now 😄
